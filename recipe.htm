Wod Fam Choc Sod

We ran out of letters for the display board, but this is our World Famous Chocolate Soda.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz soda (cola)
  • 1 Tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 1 glass cup
  • Spoon

Instructions

  1. Pour the soda into the glass cup
  2. Add the syrup into the soda
  3. Use the spoon to stir the mixture
  4. Remove the spoon before enjoying
